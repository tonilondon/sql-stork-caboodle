# Troubleshooting Tips

1. **Read the Error Message**:
   - SQL error messages often guide you to the issue.

2. **Check Your Syntax**:
   - Verify spelling of table/column names.
   - Ensure proper placement of commas and keywords.

3. **Verify the Database**:
   - Use the `USE` directive to switch databases.

Example:
```sql
USE Caboodle_Feb;

4. **Ask for Help**:
- Contact trainers or refer to documentation.
